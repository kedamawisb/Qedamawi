WHO I AM

ሰላም! I'm Kedamawi (Qedamawi or ቀዳማዊ every so often) - a Chemistry student, author, and builder based in Washington, D.C. My name means the first in Ge'ez and Amharic, and I intend to live up to it. My hobbies range from watching and playing soccer, all the way to reading philosophical and theological books. I was born and raised in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia where I spent most of my life trying to make it big playing soccer in various academies from ages 11-17.

My faith as an Oriental Orthodox Christian shapes my morals, my views, and quite literally everything I build. That said — I love to debate. I like approaching ideas by bracketing my own biases and presuppositions in order to grasp a better understanding of the subject. I am a strong believer of epistemic humility.
